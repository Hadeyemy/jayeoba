Biography

Ms ADERANTI OLUYEMI JAYEOBA was born to Pa Alfred (late) and Mrs Bola Jayeoba ( both natives of Ibadan)over fifty years ago. She attended St Paul School Oke Ibadan between 1975 and 1981 for her Primary Education. Ms Jayeoba received her Secondary School Education at Queen of Apostle Oluyoro Ibadan between 1982 and 1988. To qualify as a Professional Environmental Health Officer, Ms Jayeoba proceeded to the famous School of Hygiene Eleyele Ibadan, now College of Health and Science Technology where she obtained ND and HND. Having bagged her qualification as a certified Professional Environmental Health Officer, Ms Jayeoba secured her first appointment with you Ibadan North Local Government where she worked for few years before being transferred to Ibadan North West Local Government and later to Ibadan North East Local Government where she is presently. In her career as Environmental Health Officer, Ms Jayeoba had risen through the ranks to become Chief Environmental Health Officer. Iya Jaye as she is fondly called is a hardworking officer who has been so committed to her duty. She is a sociable personality. She is presently the President of Adorable Sisters' Stakeholders Club. Ms Aderanti Oluyemi Jayeoba is married and blessed with children.
